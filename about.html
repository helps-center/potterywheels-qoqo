About.

Pottery is super old and still around! People have been using clay to make pots, tools, and art for ages. Each piece shows off the maker's creativity and culture. The pottery wheel is key—it made working with clay way easier, letting people get precise and artistic. We're here to support that tradition, making pottery accessible and inspiring for everyone, whether you're just starting out or you're a pro.

We got into pottery because we love how hands-on, chill, and creative it is. The wheel isn't just a tool—it's how imagination turns into real stuff. Every spin needs focus and patience, connecting you to the clay. You can turn plain clay into cool things: bowls, vases, cups, plates, and sculptures that show off your style.

Pottery wheels have been around for thousands of years in cultures all over the world. From simple hand-powered wheels to today's electric ones, they've helped people make amazing stuff. Even though the wheels have changed, pottery is still about the artist and the clay working together. We support this history, teaching old techniques while also showing off what’s cool about new tools.

Our Goal

We want everyone to try pottery! We give you the info, tips, and ideas you need to really get creative. Whether you’re just learning to center clay or trying out new ways to shape and decorate, we're here to help you get good at it. We think pottery isn't just about making things—it's about learning focus and patience and feeling good about making something real from scratch.

Our Way

We look at all kinds of pottery wheels, like electric, kick, tabletop, and studio models. Each has its own vibe—power, size, speed, and stability—that changes what you can make. We want to help you find the right wheel for your skill, space, and what you want to make. We also talk about the tools you need, from shaping tools to sponges and wires. Plus, we give tips on setting up your space so it's comfy and works well.

Skills and Getting Good

Learning the pottery wheel takes skill, practice, and feeling. We help you every step of the way, from the basics to doing cool stuff like shaping, trimming, and adding handles. We show you how to perfect your shapes and add your own style. We care about skill, and also about having fun and always learning.

Pottery Ideas

Pottery isn't just art—it's a way to chill out and focus. The wheel helps you get into a rhythm, so your hands and mind work together smoothly. Working with clay helps you slow down, get real, and feel good about making something that lasts. We talk about these good things and encourage potters to see each piece as art and a personal thing.

People and Talking

We think pottery is better together. We have a place where people can share ideas, get inspired, and help each other get better. Whether it's through videos, guides, or chats, we want to make a place where people are curious and creative. By hanging out with others who love pottery, you get knowledge, support, and motivation to get even better.

Keeping it Green

We want pottery to be good for the planet. From using clay responsibly to taking care of your wheels and tools, we have tips to keep your pottery practice friendly to the Earth and to help your stuff last longer. Clean spaces, taking care of tools, and using materials wisely are important for good work and for keeping the craft going.

For Everyone, Anywhere

Pottery really is for everyone. No matter your age, skill, or where you're from, you can try pottery. Whether you're just curious, want to relax, or you're a pro trying to improve, the pottery wheel has endless chances for you to get creative. We want to make pottery easy, fun, and inspiring, so everyone can feel the magic of turning clay into something beautiful.

Why We're Here

We’re here because we love pottery! We want to help people make stuff and share the tips and ideas that make pottery fun. Every spin of the wheel is a chance to get creative and build skill. Every piece tells a story—a story of creativity, hard work, and the awesome teamwork between hands and clay.

Come with us and check out all the cool things you can do with pottery! We respect the old ways while still loving new stuff, being creative, and having a good time making. With every turn of the wheel, we want you to find your awesome, shape your ideas, and make your dreams come true with clay. It’s more than just making pots—it’s a personal thing, a team effort, and a celebration of being human.
